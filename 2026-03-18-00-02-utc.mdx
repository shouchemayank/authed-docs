## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as a Jedi Padawan under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was first introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili, Ahsoka was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Master Yoda — a pairing designed to teach Anakin the responsibility of mentorship.

### The Clone Wars

During the galactic conflict, Ahsoka proved herself as a capable and courageous leader. She commanded clone troopers in numerous battles and earned the trust of allies across the Republic. Her signature dual white lightsabers and distinctive montrals made her instantly recognizable on the battlefield.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was put on trial by the Republic military. Though she was ultimately exonerated when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true culprit, the experience shattered Ahsoka's trust in the Jedi Council. She chose to walk away from the Order — a decision that deeply affected both her and Anakin.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance, operating under the codename **Fulcrum**. She continued to fight against the dark side long after the fall of the Republic, confronting former allies and enemies alike, including a fateful encounter with Darth Vader in the Sith temple on Malachor.
