## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A legendary Jedi Master, Yoda trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a crucial role in the Galactic Republic's history.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order
- **Lifespan**: Over 900 years

### Notable traits

Yoda is recognized for his distinctive speech pattern, placing verbs at the end of sentences. His wisdom and mastery of the Force made him one of the most powerful Jedi to ever live.

<Info>
"Do or do not. There is no try." - Yoda
</Info>

### Appearances

Yoda appears throughout the Star Wars saga:

1. **The Phantom Menace** - As a senior member of the Jedi Council
2. **Attack of the Clones** - Leading clone troopers into battle
3. **Revenge of the Sith** - Confronting Emperor Palpatine
4. **The Empire Strikes Back** - Training Luke Skywalker on Dagobah
5. **Return of the Jedi** - Becoming one with the Force

### Legacy

Yoda's teachings continue to influence Jedi philosophy, emphasizing patience, wisdom, and the importance of controlling one's emotions to avoid the dark side of the Force.
