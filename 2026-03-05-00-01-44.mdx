## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple to begin her training. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Known as**: "Snips" (by Anakin), "Fulcrum" (codename in Rebellion)

### Notable characteristics

Ahsoka is known for her distinctive orange skin, white facial markings, and blue-and-white striped head-tails (lekku and montrals). She wields two lightsabers—one standard length and one shorter shoto blade—both originally green, later white after purifying Kyber crystals from an Inquisitor's weapon.

### Legacy

After leaving the Jedi Order before its fall, Ahsoka survived Order 66 and eventually became a key figure in the early Rebellion against the Empire. She adopted the codename "Fulcrum" while coordinating intelligence operations. Her journey from young Padawan to wise Force-wielder spans multiple animated series and live-action appearances.
