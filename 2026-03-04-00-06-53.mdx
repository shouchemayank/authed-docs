## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple for training.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Lightsaber color**: Originally green and yellow-green, later white

### Story arc

Ahsoka left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple. This experience shaped her perspective on the Order and its relationship with the Republic. She later became a key figure in the Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum."

### Notable appearances

- Star Wars: The Clone Wars (animated series)
- Star Wars Rebels (animated series)
- The Mandalorian (live-action)
- Ahsoka (live-action series)

Ahsoka represents one of the most complex characters in the Star Wars universe, embodying the struggle between duty, loyalty, and personal conviction.
