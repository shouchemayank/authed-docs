## Din Djarin

Din Djarin, commonly known as "The Mandalorian" or simply "Mando," is a Mandalorian bounty hunter operating in the outer reaches of the galaxy during the New Republic era. A foundling rescued as a child during the Clone Wars, he was raised in the Fighting Corps of the Mandalorian covert.

### Background

Din Djarin was born on the planet Aq Vetina. During the Galactic Republic's final years, Separatist battle droids attacked his village, killing his parents. He was rescued by a group of Mandalorian warriors from the Death Watch and taken in as a foundling, eventually swearing the Creed of the Mandalore.

### The Way of the Mandalore

As an adherent of the Children of the Watch, Din Djarin followed a strict code:

- **Never remove his helmet** in front of another living being
- **Protect foundlings** as the highest priority
- **Speak the Creed** — "This is the Way"

### Grogu

Din Djarin's life changed when he accepted a bounty for a mysterious asset — a small, Force-sensitive child of the same species as Yoda. Rather than delivering the child to Imperial remnants, Djarin chose to protect him, eventually naming the child Grogu.

<Note>
  Grogu is approximately 50 years old despite his infant-like appearance, owing to
  his species' extremely slow rate of aging.
</Note>

### Legacy

Din Djarin's journey from a solitary bounty hunter to a protective father figure represents one of the most compelling character arcs in the Star Wars galaxy. His story explores themes of identity, belonging, and the meaning of family beyond blood ties.
