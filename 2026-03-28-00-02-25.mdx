## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and trained as a Jedi Padawan under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She became one of the most compelling characters in the Star Wars universe, growing from an eager young learner into a wise and independent warrior.

### Early life

Ahsoka was born on the planet Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Jedi Grand Master Yoda — a decision intended to help Anakin learn to let go by teaching him responsibility for another.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, including engagements on Christophsis, Teth, Ryloth, and Mandalore. Her experiences on the battlefield shaped her understanding of the war and the role of the Jedi within it.

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually acquitted when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, Ahsoka chose to leave the Order — disillusioned by the Council's lack of faith in her.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to play a pivotal role in the early days of the Rebel Alliance under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy. She later confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, in a dramatic duel on Malachor. Her story continued through the era of the New Republic, where she searched for the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger.

<Note>
  Ahsoka Tano first appeared in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most popular characters in the franchise, appearing in multiple animated series and a live-action Disney+ show bearing her name.
</Note>
