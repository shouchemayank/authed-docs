## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a crime and later acquitted, she chose to leave the Jedi Order, forging her own path in the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** White (after purifying Kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor)

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, including the Battle of Christophsis, the Second Battle of Geonosis, and the Siege of Mandalore. Her experience during the war shaped her views on the Jedi Order and the Republic, ultimately leading to her departure from both.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Order, Ahsoka adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and worked as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. She provided critical support to rebel cells, including the crew of the Ghost on Lothal. Her confrontation with her former master — now Darth Vader — on Malachor remains one of the most pivotal moments in the Star Wars saga.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano has become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. First introduced in the animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008), she has appeared across multiple series including *Star Wars Rebels* and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*, where she is portrayed by Rosario Dawson.
