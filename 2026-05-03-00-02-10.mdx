## Din Djarin

Din Djarin, also known simply as "the Mandalorian" or "Mando," is a foundling raised by the Children of the Watch who became one of the most renowned bounty hunters of the New Republic era.

### Background

Orphaned during the Clone Wars when his parents were killed in a Separatist attack on Aq Vetina, Din was rescued and adopted by the Mandalorians. He swore the Creed and lived by the strict orthodoxy of his sect, never removing his helmet in the presence of another living being.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Human
- **Affiliation**: Tribe of Mandalorians (Children of the Watch), later allied with the New Republic
- **Signature armor**: Beskar steel, including a T-visor helmet
- **Notable weapons**: Amban sniper rifle, IB-94 blaster pistol, and later the Darksaber

### Journey

Din's path is defined by duty, honor, and an unexpected bond:

1. **Bounty hunter** - Operated through the Bounty Hunters' Guild taking high-risk jobs across the Outer Rim
2. **Finding Grogu** - Tasked with retrieving "the Child," he instead chose to protect him
3. **The Mandalorian Creed** - Wielded the Darksaber after defeating Moff Gideon
4. **Reuniting with Grogu** - Reunited with his adopted son and returned to Mandalore

### Legacy

Din Djarin embodies the Mandalorian code of honor while challenging the rigid interpretations of his people. His relationship with Grogu redefined what family means in the galaxy far, far away.

> "This is the Way." — Din Djarin
