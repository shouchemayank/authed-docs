## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the famed bounty hunter Jango Fett, raised as his son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Background

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back*, where Darth Vader hired him alongside other bounty hunters to track down the Millennium Falcon. He successfully captured Han Solo in carbonite and delivered him to the crime lord Jabba the Hutt.

### Notable appearances

<Steps>
  <Step title="Attack of the Clones (2002)">
    Young Boba witnesses the death of his father Jango Fett at the hands of Jedi Master Mace Windu during the Battle of Geonosis, setting him on a path of vengeance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Empire Strikes Back (1980)">
    Boba Fett tracks the Millennium Falcon to Cloud City and delivers Han Solo, frozen in carbonite, to Jabba the Hutt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return of the Jedi (1983)">
    During the battle at the Sarlacc pit on Tatooine, Boba Fett falls into the Great Pit of Carkoon after a skirmish with Luke Skywalker and Han Solo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Mandalorian (2020)">
    Boba Fett returns, having survived the Sarlacc, and allies with Din Djarin to recover his Mandalorian armor from Marshal Cobb Vanth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Book of Boba Fett (2021)">
    Boba Fett takes over Jabba the Hutt's criminal empire on Tatooine, ruling as the new Daimyo of Mos Espa alongside Fennec Shand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Despite having relatively little screen time in the original trilogy, Boba Fett became one of the most popular Star Wars characters, largely due to his mysterious demeanor and distinctive Mandalorian armor.
</Tip>
