## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After leaving the Jedi Order, she became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance and continued to fight for justice across the galaxy.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. At the young age of 14, she was assigned as Padawan to Anakin Skywalker — a pairing that would shape both of their destinies.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka grew from an eager, sometimes reckless apprentice into a capable leader and warrior. She commanded clone troopers, led critical missions, and earned the nickname **Snips** from her master. Key moments include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement alongside Anakin
- **Siege of Mandalore** — where she faced Darth Maul in one of the most iconic duels of the era
- **Order 66** — she narrowly survived the clone purge with the help of Captain Rex

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of a bombing at the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka was put on trial. Although eventually cleared, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She walked away from the Order, one of the most emotionally powerful moments in the Star Wars saga.

### The Rebellion and beyond

Operating under the codename **Fulcrum**, Ahsoka became an intelligence operative for the growing Rebel network. She later confronted her former master — now Darth Vader — in a devastating lightsaber duel on Malachor. She survived and continued her journey through the World Between Worlds and beyond, eventually searching for the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano represents growth, resilience, and moral clarity. She walks her own path — neither fully Jedi nor Sith — guided by a deep sense of right and wrong. Her story spans animated series (*The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*), a live-action series (*Ahsoka*), and feature films, making her one of the most enduring characters in the franchise.
