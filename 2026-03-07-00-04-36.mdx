## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she has grown into a pivotal figure across multiple eras of the Star Wars timeline.

### Background

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker

### Key facts

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, despite his initial reluctance to take on a Padawan. She earned the nickname "Snips" from her master due to her snippy attitude, while she called him "Skyguy."

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka left the Jedi Order—a decision that would later save her life during Order 66. She went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum."

### Notable appearances

1. *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008-2020)
2. *Star Wars Rebels* (2014-2018)
3. *The Mandalorian* (2020)
4. *Ahsoka* (2023)

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents growth, independence, and the idea that one can walk a moral path outside traditional institutions. Her white lightsabers—purified from Inquisitor crystals—symbolize her unique position as neither Jedi nor Sith.
