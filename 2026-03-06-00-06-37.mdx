## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a crime she did not commit, she left the Jedi Order, which profoundly affected both her and her master.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Former Jedi Order, Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color**: Originally green and yellow-green, later white

### Legacy

Ahsoka survived Order 66 and went into hiding, later becoming a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum." She eventually confronted her former master after his transformation into Darth Vader, in one of the most emotionally charged moments in Star Wars storytelling.

Her journey from young Padawan to independent Force-user represents one of the most complete character arcs in the Star Wars universe, appearing across animated series, live-action shows, and various other media.
