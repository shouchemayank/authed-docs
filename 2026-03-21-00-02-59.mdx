## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was first introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at the age of three on her homeworld of Shili. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she began her training in the ways of the Force.

### The Clone Wars

At the age of 14, Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Master Yoda. Despite a rocky start, the two developed a strong bond and fought side by side throughout the Clone Wars. She earned the nickname **"Snips"** from Anakin, while she called him **"Skyguy"**.

Key events during the Clone Wars include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement alongside Anakin
- **Rescue of Jabba's son** — a mission that secured Hutt cooperation with the Republic
- **Siege of Mandalore** — her final major battle of the war, where she faced Darth Maul

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She chose to walk away from the Order — a decision that deeply affected Anakin.

### The Rebellion era

After Order 66 and the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually joined the early Rebel Alliance under the codename **"Fulcrum"**, providing intelligence to various rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

Her confrontation with Darth Vader — her former master — on Malachor remains one of the most emotional moments in Star Wars history.

### Lightsabers

Ahsoka is known for wielding **two white lightsabers**, which she obtained by purifying kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor's weapon. The white blades symbolize her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.

<Note>
  Ahsoka is one of the few Force users in Star Wars canon who is neither Jedi nor Sith, walking her own path in the Force.
</Note>
