## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A member of an unknown species, this small green being served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order for centuries before the fall of the Galactic Republic.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic
- **Lightsaber color**: Green

### Notable quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Size matters not. Look at me. Judge me by my size, do you?"

### Legacy

Yoda trained Jedi for over 800 years, making him one of the most experienced Force users in galactic history. He was instrumental in training Luke Skywalker, ultimately helping to bring about the fall of the Galactic Empire. His wisdom and teachings continue to guide Jedi long after his death on Dagobah.
