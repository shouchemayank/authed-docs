## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* animated series, she has since become one of the most popular characters in the entire franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at a young age. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to begin her training in the ways of the Force.

### The Clone Wars

At the age of 14, Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Jedi Grand Master Yoda. Despite a rocky start, Ahsoka and Anakin developed a strong master-apprentice bond. She participated in many key battles throughout the Clone Wars, including:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement
- **Battle of Ryloth** — where she led a squadron against a Separatist blockade
- **Siege of Mandalore** — her final mission during the Clone Wars era

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Although she was ultimately proven innocent, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She made the difficult decision to leave, walking away from Anakin and the only life she had known.

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually joined the growing rebellion under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy.
