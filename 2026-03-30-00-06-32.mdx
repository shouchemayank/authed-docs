## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who became one of the most important figures in the galaxy's history. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan during the Clone Wars, she grew into a formidable Force-wielder and leader in her own right.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. At just 14 years old, she was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda — a pairing that surprised them both but ultimately shaped her into one of the most skilled warriors of her era.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She fought in many key battles alongside her master and Captain Rex of the 501st Legion. Her resourcefulness and courage earned the respect of clone troopers and Jedi alike.

<Note>
Ahsoka's signature fighting style uses two lightsabers — a standard-length blade and a shorter shoto blade — in the Jar'Kai dual-wielding technique.
</Note>

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience left Ahsoka disillusioned. She chose to walk away from the Jedi Order, a decision that would haunt Anakin Skywalker and arguably contribute to his fall to the dark side.

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka survived by going into hiding. She eventually resurfaced under the codename **Fulcrum**, serving as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. She provided critical support to rebel cells across the galaxy, including the crew of the starship *Ghost*.
