## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she earned the nickname "Snips" for her sharp wit and feisty personality.

### Early years

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, partly to teach Anakin the responsibility of mentorship. Despite a rocky start, the two developed a strong bond as they fought side by side across numerous battlefields during the Clone Wars. She proved herself as a capable leader, commanding clone troopers and undertaking dangerous missions.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Although she was ultimately proven innocent when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true perpetrator, the experience shattered Ahsoka's trust in the Order. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the only life she had ever known.

### Life after the Order

Following her departure, Ahsoka operated under the codename "Fulcrum" and became a key intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. She provided crucial support to rebel cells across the galaxy, including the crew of the starship Ghost on Lothal.

### Key traits

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance
- **Lightsaber color:** Green and yellow-green (as Padawan), white (after purifying Inquisitor crystals)
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker

<Note>
  Ahsoka is one of the few Force users to wield white lightsabers, which she obtained by purifying kyber crystals taken from a defeated Inquisitor.
</Note>

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano has become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars franchise. First introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she has appeared across multiple animated series and live-action productions, growing from a young Padawan into one of the galaxy's most formidable Force wielders.
