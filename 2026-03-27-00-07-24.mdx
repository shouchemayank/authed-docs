## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, a pairing that shaped both of their destinies.

### Early life and the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic, leading clone troopers in battles across the galaxy. She earned the nickname "Snips" from her master Anakin, while she called him "Skyguy." Over the course of the war, she grew from an eager and impulsive student into a skilled and confident leader.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was ultimately exonerated when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Order, one of the most pivotal moments in the prequel era.

### The Rebellion era

During the age of the Empire, Ahsoka operated under the codename "Fulcrum," providing intelligence to various rebel cells. She played a key role in the early formation of the Rebel Alliance and eventually confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, in a dramatic duel on Malachor.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano has become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe, appearing across *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*. She represents the idea that you don't need to be a Jedi or a Sith to make a difference in the galaxy — sometimes the most important path is your own.
