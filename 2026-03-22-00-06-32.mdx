## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who became one of the most important figures in the galaxy's fight against tyranny. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's apprentice during the Clone Wars, she grew from an eager young learner into a wise and independent warrior.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child on the planet Shili. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training and was eventually assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker by Grand Master Yoda during the Clone Wars.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought in numerous battles alongside Anakin and the 501st Legion, led by Captain Rex. She proved herself to be a skilled combatant and strategist, earning the nickname "Snips" from her master. Key events during this period include:

- The Battle of Christophsis, her first major engagement
- The rescue of Jabba the Hutt's son, Rotta
- The Siege of Mandalore, where she helped capture Darth Maul

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was ultimately exonerated when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order. This decision profoundly shaped her identity and philosophy going forward.

### The Rebellion era

After the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka operated under the codename "Fulcrum," helping to organize and support early rebel cells. She became a key intelligence operative for the growing Rebel Alliance and eventually confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, in a dramatic duel on the Sith temple of Malachor.

### Legacy

Ahsoka's journey represents one of the most compelling character arcs in Star Wars. She embodies the idea that you don't need to be a Jedi to fight for what's right. Her dual white lightsabers symbolize her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith, walking her own path in the Force.

<Note>
Ahsoka Tano first appeared in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since appeared in multiple animated series and the live-action series *Ahsoka* on Disney+.
