## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter and the unaltered clone of Jango Fett, he was raised as his father's son on the ocean world of Kamino.

### Background

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back* (1980), where he tracked Han Solo to Cloud City on behalf of Darth Vader. He became one of the most feared bounty hunters in the galaxy, known for his distinctive Mandalorian armor and ruthless efficiency.

### Notable appearances

- **The Empire Strikes Back** — Hired by Darth Vader to track the Millennium Falcon
- **Return of the Jedi** — Fell into the Sarlacc pit at the Great Pit of Carkoon
- **The Mandalorian** — Returned to reclaim his armor from Din Djarin
- **The Book of Boba Fett** — Took over Jabba the Hutt's criminal empire on Tatooine

### Legacy

Despite limited screen time in the original trilogy, Boba Fett became a fan favorite largely due to his mysterious persona and imposing armor design. His survival of the Sarlacc pit was confirmed in *The Mandalorian*, leading to his own spin-off series where he transitioned from bounty hunter to crime lord ruling Mos Espa with a code of honor.
