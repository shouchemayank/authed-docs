## Din Djarin

Din Djarin, known to many as "The Mandalorian" or simply "Mando," is a human male bounty hunter who operates during the era of the New Republic. A foundling raised in the Mandalorian creed, he follows the strict code of the Children of the Watch — a traditionalist faction that forbids removing one's helmet in the presence of others.

### Background

Din Djarin was orphaned as a child during the Clone Wars when Separatist battle droids attacked his homeworld. He was rescued and adopted by Mandalorian warriors of the Death Watch, who raised him in their warrior traditions. He eventually became one of the most skilled bounty hunters in the Outer Rim.

### The child

Djarin's life changed when he accepted a bounty from an Imperial remnant led by Moff Gideon. The target turned out to be a Force-sensitive child of Yoda's species — later named Grogu. Instead of delivering the child, Djarin chose to protect him, becoming a fugitive and setting off on a journey across the galaxy.

<Note>
  Din Djarin is one of the few Mandalorians to have wielded the Darksaber, an ancient weapon that grants its bearer a claim to rule Mandalore.
</Note>

### Key traits

- **Combat expertise** — Proficient with blasters, melee weapons, and Mandalorian gadgets like the whistling birds and flamethrower gauntlet.
- **Beskar armor** — His full suit of beskar steel armor is nearly indestructible and was forged by the Armorer of his covert.
- **The Way** — He strictly adheres to the Mandalorian creed, famously declaring "This is the Way."
- **Loyalty** — Despite his solitary nature, Djarin forms deep bonds with allies like Grogu, Cara Dune, and Bo-Katan Kryze.
