## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at the age of three and raised in the Jedi Temple. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, earning the nickname "Snips" for her feisty attitude.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** Initially green, later white

### Legacy

After leaving the Jedi Order due to false accusations, Ahsoka became a key figure in the early Rebellion against the Galactic Empire. She adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and helped coordinate rebel cells across the galaxy.

Her signature dual white lightsabers represent her unique position—no longer a Jedi, yet still walking in the light. Ahsoka remains one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe, embodying resilience, independence, and unwavering moral conviction.
