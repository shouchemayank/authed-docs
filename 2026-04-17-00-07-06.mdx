## Din Djarin — The Mandalorian

Din Djarin, commonly known as "The Mandalorian" or "Mando," is a human male Mandalorian warrior and bounty hunter active during the era of the New Republic. Operating as a lone gunfighter, he became one of the most skilled hunters in the Outer Rim.

### Background

Din Djarin was orphaned during the Clone Wars when Separatist battle droids attacked his homeworld. He was rescued and adopted by members of the Mandalorian culture, specifically the Children of the Watch — a sect that adhered to the ancient Way of the Mandalore.

### Key traits

- **Armor**: Wears full beskar Mandalorian armor, making him nearly impervious to blaster fire and even lightsaber strikes
- **Creed**: Followed the Way of the Mandalore, which forbade him from removing his helmet in front of others
- **Weapons**: Skilled with a wide arsenal including a disintegration rifle, whistling birds, and a beskar spear
- **Ship**: Originally piloted the Razor Crest before later acquiring a modified Naboo N-1 starfighter

### The quest for Grogu

Din Djarin's life changed when he accepted a bounty from a mysterious Imperial remnant client. The target turned out to be Grogu — a Force-sensitive child of the same species as Yoda. Rather than deliver the child, Djarin broke the bounty hunter's code and became Grogu's protector, forging a father-son bond that drove the events of the entire series.
