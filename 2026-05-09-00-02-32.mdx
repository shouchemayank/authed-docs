## Cad Bane

Cad Bane is one of the most feared and skilled bounty hunters in the Star Wars galaxy. A Duros from the planet Duro, he rose to prominence during the waning years of the Galactic Republic and became the gold standard for bounty hunters of his era.

### Background

After the death of Jango Fett, Cad Bane filled the void as the galaxy's most notorious bounty hunter. Operating with cold precision and unmatched cunning, he took on jobs from crime lords, Separatists, and even Darth Sidious himself.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Duros
- **Signature look**: Wide-brimmed hat, long duster coat, and breathing tubes
- **Weapons of choice**: Twin LL-30 blaster pistols and an array of gadgets
- **Notable traits**: Calm under pressure, ruthless professionalism, and a fierce sense of personal honor among hunters

### Notable exploits

1. **The Senate hostage crisis** - Led a daring raid on the Galactic Senate to free Ziro the Hutt
2. **Holocron heist** - Infiltrated the Jedi Temple to steal a Jedi holocron for Darth Sidious
3. **Kidnapping Force-sensitive children** - Carried out one of his most morally questionable jobs for Sidious
4. **Confrontation with Boba Fett** - His final showdown on Tatooine became legendary among bounty hunters

### Legacy

Cad Bane influenced an entire generation of bounty hunters, including a young Boba Fett who saw him as both a mentor and rival. His methods, gear, and reputation set the bar for what it meant to be a professional in the trade.

> "I'm the best bounty hunter in the galaxy. Don't you forget it." — Cad Bane
