## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who was trained by Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order and forged her own path through the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** White (dual-wielding)

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic alongside her master Anakin Skywalker and Clone Captain Rex. She participated in many critical battles including the Battle of Christophsis, the Second Battle of Geonosis, and the Siege of Mandalore.

### After the Jedi Order

Following her departure from the Order, Ahsoka adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and became an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. She played a pivotal role in organizing rebel cells across the galaxy, working closely with Bail Organa to coordinate resistance against the Empire.

### Legacy

Ahsoka is one of the most significant Force users in galactic history to operate outside the traditional Jedi structure. Her journey from Padawan to independent warrior highlights that you don't need to follow the conventional path to make a difference. She wielded distinctive white lightsabers, symbolizing her neutrality — neither Jedi nor Sith.
