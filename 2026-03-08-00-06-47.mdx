## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple for training at a young age. She became the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she proved herself as a skilled warrior and leader.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order, Galactic Republic, Rebel Alliance
- **Weapons:** Dual lightsabers (originally green, later white)

### Notable achievements

1. Led Republic forces in numerous Clone Wars battles
2. Left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of terrorism
3. Survived Order 66 and went into hiding
4. Became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum"
5. Confronted her former master Darth Vader on Malachor

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents one of the most compelling character arcs in Star Wars. Her journey from eager Padawan to independent Force user outside the Jedi Order explores themes of identity, loyalty, and finding one's own path. She remains a symbol of hope and resilience in the galaxy.
