## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the legendary Jango Fett, raised as his son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Background

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back*, where Darth Vader hired him alongside other bounty hunters to track down Han Solo and the Millennium Falcon. He successfully delivered Solo, frozen in carbonite, to the crime lord Jabba the Hutt on Tatooine.

### Legacy

After surviving the Sarlacc pit on Tatooine, Boba Fett eventually claimed the throne of the late Jabba the Hutt, becoming the new Daimyo of Mos Espa. His story of resilience and reinvention made him a fan favorite across generations of Star Wars media.
