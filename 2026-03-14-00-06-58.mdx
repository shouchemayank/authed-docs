## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner to Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was assigned to Anakin by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, and despite a rocky start, the two developed a close bond.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Weapon**: Dual lightsabers (white blades)

### Character journey

Ahsoka left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple. Though eventually cleared of the charges, she chose not to return, feeling betrayed by the Council's lack of faith in her.

She later became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance, operating under the codename "Fulcrum" and helping to coordinate resistance cells against the Empire.
