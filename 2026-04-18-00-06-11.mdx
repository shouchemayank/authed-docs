## Din Djarin — The Mandalorian

Din Djarin, commonly known as "The Mandalorian" or "Mando," is a Mandalorian bounty hunter operating in the outer reaches of the galaxy after the fall of the Galactic Empire.

### Background

Din Djarin was orphaned as a child during the Clone Wars when Separatist battle droids attacked his home. He was rescued and adopted by members of the Death Watch, a Mandalorian warrior sect, who raised him in the Fighting Corps and trained him in their ways.

### Key traits

- **Occupation:** Bounty hunter and later protector of Grogu
- **Armor:** Wears full beskar Mandalorian armor, which is nearly indestructible
- **Weapons:** Amban phase-pulse blaster, whistling birds, vibro-knife, and a beskar spear
- **Ship:** Originally the Razor Crest, later acquired a modified Naboo N-1 starfighter
- **Creed:** Follows the Way of the Mandalore — he originally never removed his helmet in front of others

### The quest with Grogu

Din Djarin's life changed when he was hired to retrieve a bounty target known as "The Child" — a young Force-sensitive being of Yoda's species later named Grogu. Instead of delivering the child to an Imperial remnant, Djarin chose to protect Grogu, becoming his adoptive father and setting off on a journey across the galaxy to reunite the child with the Jedi.

> "This is the Way." — Din Djarin
