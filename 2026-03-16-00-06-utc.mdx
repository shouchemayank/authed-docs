## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is a senior member of the Jedi Council and one of the most powerful Jedi in the history of the Galactic Republic. Known for his stern demeanor and unwavering commitment to the Jedi Order, he serves as a key military leader during the Clone Wars.

### Notable traits

- **Lightsaber color:** Purple — the only Jedi known to wield an amethyst-bladed lightsaber
- **Fighting style:** Master of Vaapad, the most aggressive form of lightsaber combat (Form VII), which channels the user's inner darkness into a weapon of the light
- **Rank:** Senior member of the Jedi High Council
- **Homeworld:** Haruun Kal

### Key moments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery of Anakin Skywalker">
    Windu was among the first to express skepticism about training Anakin Skywalker, sensing great danger in the boy's future. His caution proved tragically prophetic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Battle of Geonosis">
    He led a Jedi strike team of over 200 Jedi to Geonosis, igniting the first battle of the Clone Wars. During the arena battle, he personally defeated the bounty hunter Jango Fett.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confrontation with Palpatine">
    Windu confronted Chancellor Palpatine after learning his true identity as Darth Sidious. He defeated Palpatine in lightsaber combat but was ultimately betrayed by Anakin Skywalker and thrown from a window of the Senate building.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Legacy

Despite his fall, Mace Windu's legacy endures as a symbol of the Jedi Order's dedication to justice. His mastery of the Force and lightsaber combat remain legendary, and his warnings about the dangers of the dark side proved devastatingly accurate.

<Note>
  Mace Windu is portrayed by Samuel L. Jackson in the Star Wars prequel trilogy (Episodes I–III).
</Note>
