## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is a senior member of the Jedi Council and one of the most powerful Jedi in the history of the Galactic Republic. Known for his stern demeanor and unmatched combat skills, he wields a distinctive purple lightsaber — the only one of its kind among the Jedi Order.

### Background

Born on the planet Haruun Kal, Windu was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. He rose through the ranks to become one of the youngest members ever appointed to the Jedi High Council.

### Combat style

Windu is the sole master of Vaapad, a variant of Form VII lightsaber combat that channels the user's inner darkness into a weapon of light. This aggressive fighting style treads dangerously close to the dark side, but Windu's discipline and moral clarity allow him to use it without succumbing to corruption.

### Role in the Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Windu served as a Jedi General, leading Republic forces in several key battles including the Battle of Geonosis and the liberation of Ryloth. He remained a central figure in the Council's decision-making throughout the conflict.

### Notable traits

- **Lightsaber color:** Purple (amethyst blade)
- **Homeworld:** Haruun Kal
- **Rank:** Jedi Master, Senior Council Member
- **Lightsaber form:** Vaapad (Form VII variant)
- **Portrayed by:** Samuel L. Jackson in the prequel trilogy

Mace Windu famously confronted Chancellor Palpatine after discovering his true identity as Darth Sidious, in one of the most pivotal scenes of the Star Wars saga.
