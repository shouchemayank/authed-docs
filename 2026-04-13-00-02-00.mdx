## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Grand Master Yoda, a pairing that would shape both of their destinies.

### The Clone Wars

During the galactic conflict, Ahsoka proved herself as a capable and resourceful leader. She commanded clone troopers in numerous battles across the galaxy, earning the respect of soldiers and Jedi alike. Her experiences during the war matured her far beyond her years.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though eventually cleared of all charges, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the only life she had ever known.

### Key traits

- **Resourceful** — Ahsoka consistently finds creative solutions under pressure
- **Independent** — She forges her own path outside of traditional Jedi doctrine
- **Loyal** — Despite leaving the Order, she remains committed to protecting the innocent
- **Skilled duelist** — Known for her signature reverse-grip dual-wielding lightsaber style, using white blades after purifying kyber crystals

### Legacy

Ahsoka's journey from Padawan to independent Force-wielder represents one of the richest character arcs in Star Wars storytelling. She bridges the prequel and original trilogies, playing a key role in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum." Her story continues to expand across animated series and live-action appearances.
