## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to be trained. She was later assigned as the Padawan learner to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was born on the planet Shili and was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age. After being brought to the Jedi Order, she trained at the Jedi Temple until she was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Master Yoda. Her assignment was unconventional — Yoda believed that by giving Anakin a Padawan, he would learn to let go of his attachments.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and Captain Rex of the 501st Legion across numerous battlefields. She proved herself as a capable and resourceful warrior, earning the nickname **"Snips"** from her master, while she called him **"Skyguy"**.

Key moments during the Clone Wars include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — Her first major engagement as Anakin's Padawan
- **Siege of Mandalore** — One of the final battles of the Clone Wars, where she faced Maul
- **Leaving the Jedi Order** — After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka chose to leave the Order even after being cleared of the charges

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually resurfaced as **"Fulcrum"**, an intelligence operative working for the early Rebel Alliance under Bail Organa. In this role, she provided critical support to rebel cells across the galaxy.

### Lightsabers

Ahsoka is known for wielding two lightsabers in a distinctive reverse grip:

- During the Clone Wars, she carried **green and yellow-green** lightsabers
- After the fall of the Republic, she constructed a pair of **white** lightsabers using kyber crystals purified from an Inquisitor's blade

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano has become one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe, appearing across multiple animated series including *The Clone Wars* and *Rebels*, as well as the live-action series *Ahsoka* on Disney+. She represents the journey of someone who walks a path outside the traditional Jedi Order while still serving the light side of the Force.
