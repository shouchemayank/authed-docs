## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she has grown into a fan-favorite character with her own live-action series.

### Background

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliations**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Lightsaber color**: White (dual-wielded)

### Key facts

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, and their bond became one of the most compelling relationships in Star Wars storytelling. She left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of a crime, which deeply affected both her and Anakin.

After Order 66, Ahsoka survived and eventually became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance, operating under the codename "Fulcrum." She provided intelligence to various rebel cells, including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Notable appearances

1. **The Clone Wars** (2008-2020) - Animated series where she was introduced
2. **Rebels** (2014-2018) - Animated series where she returned as Fulcrum
3. **The Mandalorian** (2020) - Her live-action debut
4. **Ahsoka** (2023) - Her own live-action series

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents the idea that one can still follow the light side of the Force without being part of the Jedi Order. Her journey from impulsive Padawan to wise warrior has inspired countless fans.
