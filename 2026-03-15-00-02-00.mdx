## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who first appeared in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker. Over the course of her journey, she grew from an impulsive young apprentice into one of the most skilled and principled Force users in the galaxy.

### Background

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple at a young age. She was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan during the Clone Wars, where she proved herself in numerous battles across the galaxy.

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order — a decision that shaped her identity for years to come. She later resurfaced as the operative known as **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to the growing Rebel Alliance.

### Why she matters

- She demonstrates that you don't need to belong to the Jedi Order to follow the light side of the Force.
- Her departure from the Order foreshadowed the institution's eventual downfall.
- She serves as a bridge character across *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*.
