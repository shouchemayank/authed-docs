## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. She was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she quickly proved herself as a capable and resourceful warrior.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was born on the planet Shili and was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age. After being brought to the Jedi Order, she trained under the guidance of various Jedi Masters before being assigned to Anakin Skywalker. Their bond became one of the most significant relationships in the final years of the Jedi Order.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She participated in numerous battles across the galaxy, including engagements on Christophsis, Teth, Ryloth, and Mandalore. Her experience on the front lines shaped her understanding of the war's true cost and the growing corruption within the Republic.

<Note>
Ahsoka is one of the few characters to appear across multiple Star Wars eras, from the prequel-era Clone Wars through the original trilogy period and beyond.
</Note>

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She chose to walk away, becoming one of the few to voluntarily leave the Jedi.

### Life after the Order

Following Order 66 and the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually resurfaced as the operative known as "Fulcrum," working with the early rebel networks to fight the Galactic Empire. Her intelligence work was instrumental in the formation of the broader Rebel Alliance.

### Key traits

- **Dual white lightsabers** — After leaving the Jedi Order, Ahsoka wielded a pair of white-bladed lightsabers, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.
- **Strategic thinker** — Ahsoka consistently demonstrated sharp tactical instincts, often finding creative solutions in desperate situations.
- **Moral compass** — She maintained a strong sense of justice even when it put her at odds with powerful institutions.
