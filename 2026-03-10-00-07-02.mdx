## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, earning the nickname "Snips" for her feisty personality.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color**: Green and yellow-green (as Padawan), white (after leaving the Order)

### Notable achievements

Ahsoka proved herself as a capable warrior and leader throughout the Clone Wars. She commanded troops in numerous battles and developed a close bond with clone troopers like Captain Rex. After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she left the Order—a decision that ultimately saved her life when Order 66 was executed.

She later became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum," providing intelligence to various rebel cells. Her story continued through the age of the New Republic, where she searched for answers about the Force and her former master's fate.

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents a unique perspective in the Star Wars universe—a former Jedi who walks her own path, neither fully aligned with the light side's rigid structure nor tempted by the dark side. Her journey from eager Padawan to wise warrior has made her one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.
