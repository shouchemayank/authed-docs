## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is one of the most powerful Jedi Masters in Star Wars history, renowned for his exceptional combat skills and distinctive purple lightsaber.

### Background

Born on the planet Haruun Kal, Mace Windu was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. He rose through the ranks to become a senior member of the Jedi High Council during the final years of the Galactic Republic.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Human
- **Lightsaber color**: Purple (unique among Jedi)
- **Fighting style**: Vaapad, a form he created that channels inner darkness
- **Notable traits**: Stoic demeanor, unwavering commitment to the Jedi Code, exceptional dueling skills

### Accomplishments

Mace Windu's legacy includes many achievements:

1. **Jedi Council member** - One of the most respected voices on the Jedi High Council
2. **Battle of Geonosis** - Led the Jedi strike team that sparked the Clone Wars
3. **Vaapad creator** - Developed a lightsaber form that only he could master without falling to the dark side
4. **Confrontation with Palpatine** - Defeated Darth Sidious in lightsaber combat before being betrayed

### Fighting philosophy

Mace Windu's Vaapad style was revolutionary yet dangerous. It required the user to channel their own darkness into their fighting without being consumed by it. Only Windu had the discipline to use it safely.
