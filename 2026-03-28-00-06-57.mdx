## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was first introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* animated film in 2008 and quickly became one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at age three. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she trained as a youngling before being assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his Padawan — a decision made by Yoda to help Anakin learn to let go of attachment.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka proved herself a capable and resourceful warrior. She fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, including:

- The Battle of Christophsis
- The rescue of Jabba the Hutt's son, Rotta
- The Second Battle of Geonosis
- The Siege of Mandalore

Her callsign "Snips" was given to her by Anakin, reflecting her snippy attitude early on, while she called him "Skyguy."

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though ultimately exonerated when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true culprit, the experience shattered Ahsoka's trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the only life she had ever known.

### The Rebellion era

Ahsoka resurfaced during the age of the Galactic Empire under the codename **Fulcrum**, operating as an intelligence agent for the growing Rebel Alliance. She provided critical information and support to rebel cells, including the crew of the *Ghost*.

Her most iconic moment came during her confrontation with Darth Vader — her former master — on Malachor, where she declared: *"I am no Jedi."*

### Legacy

Ahsoka's journey from eager Padawan to independent Force-wielder makes her one of the most compelling characters in Star Wars. She represents growth, resilience, and the idea that you can walk your own path even when the institutions you trusted let you down.

<Note>
Ahsoka Tano has appeared across multiple Star Wars media, including *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka* on Disney+.
</Note>
