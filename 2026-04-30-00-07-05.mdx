## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili, introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008) as the Padawan apprentice to Anakin Skywalker. She is one of the most beloved characters in the *Star Wars* universe, known for her growth from an impulsive young Jedi to a wise and independent warrior.

### Background

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsabers:** Dual white-bladed lightsabers
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance, Fulcrum

### Notable moments

After being wrongfully accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka left the Jedi Order, a pivotal moment that shaped her into a more nuanced figure outside the rigid Jedi code. She survived Order 66, went into hiding, and later resurfaced as the Rebel agent known as **Fulcrum**.

She has since appeared in *Star Wars Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, *The Book of Boba Fett*, and her own self-titled series, *Ahsoka*, where she is portrayed in live-action by Rosario Dawson.
