## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. She was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she quickly proved herself as a capable and resourceful warrior.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order, Galactic Republic, Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** Initially green, later white

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic alongside her master Anakin Skywalker and Captain Rex. She participated in numerous battles across the galaxy, including the Battle of Christophsis, the siege of Mandalore, and many other pivotal engagements.

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was ultimately proven innocent when Barriss Offee confessed, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order — a decision that deeply affected her former master.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum," providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy. She is widely regarded as one of the most compelling characters in the Star Wars saga, bridging the prequel and original trilogy eras.
