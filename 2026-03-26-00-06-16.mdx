## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order and forged her own path through the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** White (after purifying Kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor)

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan during the Battle of Christophsis. Despite a rocky start, the two developed a strong bond. She fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, including the Siege of Mandalore, where she helped capture Darth Maul just as Order 66 was executed.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Order, Ahsoka operated under the codename "Fulcrum" and became a key intelligence operative for the growing Rebel Alliance. She provided critical information to rebel cells across the galaxy while keeping her identity hidden.

### Legacy

Ahsoka stands out among Star Wars characters for walking a path between the Jedi and the Sith. She wields white lightsabers, symbolizing her neutrality and independence from both orders. Her journey from Padawan to independent Force-wielder makes her one of the most compelling characters in the Star Wars universe.
