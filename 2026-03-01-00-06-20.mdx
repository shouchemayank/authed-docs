## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. Originally introduced in the animated series *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she has grown from Anakin Skywalker's Padawan learner into a legendary figure in her own right.

### Background

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker

### Key characteristics

Ahsoka is known for her distinctive orange skin, white facial markings, and blue-and-white striped head-tails (lekku). She wields two white lightsabers, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and Sith orders.

### Legacy

After leaving the Jedi Order, Ahsoka adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and became a key intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. Her journey from young Padawan to independent Force-wielder represents one of the most compelling character arcs in Star Wars storytelling.

Ahsoka Tano was voiced by Ashley Eckstein in the animated series and portrayed by Rosario Dawson in live-action appearances.
