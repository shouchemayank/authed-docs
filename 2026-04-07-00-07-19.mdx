## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she quickly proved herself as a capable and courageous warrior.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, partly to teach Skywalker a sense of responsibility. Despite a rocky start, the two formed a strong bond. She fought in many key battles of the Clone Wars, including the Battle of Christophsis, the rescue of Jabba the Hutt's son, and the Siege of Mandalore.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Although she was eventually cleared when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true culprit, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She chose to walk away, marking one of the most pivotal moments in the prequel era.

### Life after the Order

Following Order 66 and the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually resurfaced as the mysterious Rebel operative known as **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to various Rebel cells. Her confrontation with her former master — now Darth Vader — on the Sith temple on Malachor remains one of the most emotional moments in the Star Wars saga.
