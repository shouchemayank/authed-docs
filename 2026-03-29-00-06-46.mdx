## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she grew from an eager and headstrong apprentice into one of the most skilled and principled Force wielders in the galaxy.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She was assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his Padawan by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, a pairing intended to teach Anakin the value of letting go.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought in numerous battles alongside Anakin and the 501st Legion. She proved herself as a capable leader and warrior, earning the respect of clone troopers like Captain Rex. Her dual-wielding lightsaber style, using a standard green blade and a shorter yellow-green shoto blade, became her signature in combat.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although her name was eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered Ahsoka's trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Order, telling Anakin she had to find her own path.

<Note>
  Ahsoka's departure from the Jedi Order is widely considered one of the most pivotal moments in *The Clone Wars* series and foreshadows the broader failings of the Jedi that led to their downfall.
</Note>

### The Rebellion era

After the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka operated under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to various rebel cells. She eventually joined the crew of the *Ghost* in their fight against the Empire, wielding a pair of white lightsabers that symbolized her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.

### Key traits

- **Resourceful** — Ahsoka consistently adapts to impossible situations, whether facing Sith Lords or navigating galactic politics.
- **Independent** — Her decision to leave the Jedi Order defined her character and set her apart from other Force users.
- **Loyal** — Despite walking her own path, Ahsoka never abandoned her friends or the fight against tyranny.
