## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta who was once the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she grew from a headstrong youngling into one of the most capable warriors in the galaxy.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. At the age of 14, she was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan — a pairing that surprised them both but ultimately forged a deep bond.

### The Clone Wars

Throughout the Clone Wars, Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She fought in key battles on Christophsis, Ryloth, and Mandalore. Her experiences on the front lines matured her quickly, and she earned the respect of clone troopers like Captain Rex.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually cleared when Barriss Offee confessed, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She chose to walk away from the Order — a decision that deeply affected Anakin.

### After the fall of the Republic

During the Imperial era, Ahsoka operated under the codename **Fulcrum**, feeding intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy. She became a key figure in the early Rebellion, working alongside leaders like Bail Organa and Hera Syndulla.

### Legacy

Ahsoka wields two white lightsabers, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith. She is neither fully Jedi nor fully something else — she walks her own path. Her story spans animated series, novels, and her own live-action series on Disney+.

<Note>
Ahsoka's white lightsaber crystals were purified from the red crystals of a defeated Inquisitor, representing her rejection of the dark side without fully returning to the Jedi Order.
</Note>
