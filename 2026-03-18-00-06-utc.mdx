## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as a Jedi Padawan under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was originally introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at age three and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. At fourteen, she was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan — a pairing that surprised them both but forged one of the strongest bonds in the Jedi Order.

### The Clone Wars

During the galactic conflict, Ahsoka proved herself as a capable leader and skilled fighter. She commanded clone troopers, undertook dangerous missions, and grew from an impulsive student into a mature warrior. Her dual-wielding lightsaber style — using a standard green blade and a shorter yellow-green shoto — became her signature in combat.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was ultimately cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult choice to walk away from the only life she had known.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to play a critical role in the founding of the Rebel Alliance under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy. She later confronted her former master — now Darth Vader — in a dramatic duel on Malachor. Her story continued through *Star Wars Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*.

<Info>
  Ahsoka's white lightsabers, first seen in *Rebels*, symbolize her independence from both the Jedi and Sith traditions. She purified the kyber crystals from an Inquisitor's lightsaber, turning them from red to white.
</Info>
