## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the infamous Jango Fett, raised as his son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Origins

Boba was created on Kamino as part of the deal between Jango Fett and the Sith Lord Count Dooku. Unlike the millions of clone troopers produced for the Grand Army of the Republic, Boba was unmodified — no accelerated aging, no behavioral conditioning. Jango raised him as his own, training him in combat, tracking, and survival from an early age.

After witnessing his father's death at the hands of Jedi Master Mace Windu during the Battle of Geonosis, Boba swore vengeance and began his journey toward becoming the galaxy's most feared bounty hunter.

### Career highlights

- **Hired by Darth Vader** to track down the Millennium Falcon, leading to the capture of Han Solo on Cloud City.
- **Delivered Han Solo to Jabba the Hutt**, collecting one of the largest bounties in the Outer Rim.
- **Survived the Sarlacc Pit** on Tatooine, defying what most considered a certain death.
- **Claimed the throne of Jabba's Palace**, eventually ruling as Daimyo of Mos Espa on Tatooine.

### Legacy

Boba Fett's influence extends far beyond his screen time. Despite having only a handful of lines in the original trilogy, he became a fan favorite and eventually received his own series, *The Book of Boba Fett*, which explored his transformation from ruthless bounty hunter to a leader seeking to rule Tatooine with respect rather than fear.
