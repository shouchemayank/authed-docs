## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple at a young age. She became the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, earning the nickname "Snips" for her feisty and snippy attitude.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order, Galactic Republic, Rebel Alliance

### Notable achievements

1. **Clone Wars veteran** - Fought in numerous battles alongside her master Anakin Skywalker and Clone Captain Rex
2. **Left the Jedi Order** - After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she chose to walk away from the Order even after being cleared
3. **Fulcrum agent** - Became a key intelligence operative for the Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum"
4. **Survived Order 66** - One of the few Force users to survive the Great Jedi Purge
5. **Confronted Darth Vader** - Faced her former master on Malachor in an emotional duel

### Famous quote

> "I am no Jedi."

Ahsoka remains one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe, representing growth, independence, and moral clarity outside the traditional Jedi path.
