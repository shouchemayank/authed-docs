## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served as Anakin Skywalker's apprentice during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order — a pivotal moment that deepened her independence and shaped her future.

### Why she matters

- **Bridge between eras** — Ahsoka connects the prequel trilogy, the original trilogy, and the post-Empire era through appearances in *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series.
- **Left the Jedi Order** — Her departure from the Order highlighted the institution's flaws well before its fall, foreshadowing the events of *Revenge of the Sith*.
- **Fulcrum** — She operated under the codename "Fulcrum" as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance, passing critical information to cells across the galaxy.
- **Survived Order 66** — Unlike most Force users, Ahsoka survived the Jedi Purge and continued to fight against the Empire from the shadows.

<Note>
  Ahsoka was originally created by George Lucas and Dave Filoni for the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and is voiced by Ashley Eckstein in the animated series.
