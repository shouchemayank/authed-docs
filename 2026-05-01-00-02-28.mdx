## Featured character: Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta Force-wielder who first appeared in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* as the young Padawan apprentice to Anakin Skywalker. Known for her dual lightsabers and acrobatic combat style, she eventually left the Jedi Order after being wrongfully accused of a crime and losing faith in the Council.

### Quick facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Signature weapons:** Twin white lightsabers
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance, Fulcrum network

After leaving the Jedi, Ahsoka took on the codename "Fulcrum" and became a key intelligence operative for the early rebellion against the Galactic Empire, later mentoring a new generation including Ezra Bridger and Sabine Wren.
