## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and went on to become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at a young age. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training and was eventually assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his apprentice by Jedi Grand Master Yoda.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka proved herself as a capable and courageous warrior. She fought in numerous battles alongside her master and the clone troopers of the 501st Legion. Over the course of the war, she matured from a headstrong young Padawan into a skilled and thoughtful leader.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually acquitted when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true perpetrator, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Jedi, one of the most pivotal moments in Clone Wars storytelling.

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually resurfaced under the codename **Fulcrum**, working as an intelligence operative for the early rebel network. Her contributions were instrumental in the formation of the Rebel Alliance.

### Key traits

- **Dual-wielding lightsabers** — Ahsoka is known for fighting with two white lightsabers, which she purified from red Inquisitor kyber crystals.
- **Strong moral compass** — She consistently follows what she believes is right, even when it puts her at odds with authority.
- **Independence** — Her decision to leave the Jedi Order defined her character arc and set her apart from traditional Jedi.

### Appearances

Ahsoka has appeared across multiple Star Wars media, including:

- *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (animated series)
- *Star Wars Rebels* (animated series)
- *The Mandalorian* (live-action, portrayed by Rosario Dawson)
- *Ahsoka* (live-action series)
