## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano was first introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan during the Clone Wars. Originally assigned to Anakin by Jedi Master Yoda, she quickly proved herself as a capable and resourceful warrior.

### Background

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka left the Jedi Order despite being cleared of all charges. This decision shaped her independent path through the galaxy and foreshadowed the Order's eventual downfall.

During the age of the Empire, she operated under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to early rebel cells. She later confronted her former master — now Darth Vader — in a duel on Malachor.

<Note>
  Ahsoka is one of the few characters to appear across animated series, live-action shows, and feature films in the Star Wars franchise.
</Note>
