## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A legendary Jedi Master, he trained Jedi for over 800 years before the fall of the Galactic Republic.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2 ft 2 in)
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Lifespan**: Approximately 900 years
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

### Notable traits

Yoda is known for his distinctive speech pattern, inverting the typical subject-verb-object order of sentences. His wisdom and mastery of the Force made him one of the most powerful Jedi to ever live.

### Famous quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Size matters not. Look at me. Judge me by my size, do you?"

### Legacy

Despite the fall of the Jedi Order, Yoda played a crucial role in training Luke Skywalker, who would eventually help redeem Darth Vader and bring balance to the Force.
