## Grogu

Grogu, affectionately nicknamed "Baby Yoda" by fans, is a Force-sensitive being from the same mysterious species as Jedi Master Yoda. He rose to global fame as the breakout character of "The Mandalorian" series.

### Background

Grogu was rescued from the Jedi Temple on Coruscant during Order 66, where he had been training as a youngling. For decades, he was hidden away to protect him from the Empire, until he was tracked down by the bounty hunter Din Djarin, who became his protector and adoptive father figure.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Unknown (same as Yoda and Yaddle)
- **Age**: Approximately 50 years old (his species ages slowly)
- **Force ability**: Highly sensitive, capable of telekinesis and Force healing
- **Notable traits**: Curious, mischievous, deeply empathetic, and surprisingly powerful

### Journey

Grogu's path is one of discovery and family:

1. **Jedi youngling** - Began Force training at the Jedi Temple before Order 66
2. **In hiding** - Spent years concealed from Imperial forces
3. **Found by Mando** - Rescued and protected by Din Djarin, forming an unbreakable bond
4. **Training with Luke** - Briefly trained with Luke Skywalker before choosing Mandalorian life
5. **Mandalorian foundling** - Officially adopted into Mandalorian culture as Din Grogu

### Legacy

Grogu represents hope, innocence, and the enduring power of chosen family. His story bridges the Jedi and Mandalorian traditions, offering a fresh perspective on what it means to belong.
