## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* animated series, she has since become one of the most iconic characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at a young age. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she began her training in the ways of the Force.

### The Clone Wars

At the age of 14, Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan — a decision made by Jedi Grand Master Yoda to help Anakin learn responsibility. Throughout the war, she proved herself as a capable leader and skilled fighter, participating in key battles across the galaxy.

- **Lightsaber style** — Ahsoka wielded two lightsabers, using a reverse grip (Shien) fighting style with a standard green blade and a shorter yellow-green shoto blade.
- **Key battles** — She fought in the Battle of Christophsis, the Siege of Mandalore, and many other critical engagements.
- **Leaving the Order** — After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order even after being acquitted, disillusioned with the Council's lack of trust.

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually joined the early rebellion under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Legacy

Ahsoka's story spans multiple eras of Star Wars storytelling — from *The Clone Wars* to *Rebels*, and into live-action with her appearance in *The Mandalorian* and her own series, *Ahsoka*. She represents the idea that you can walk away from a rigid institution and still fight for what is right.

<Note>
  Ahsoka's white lightsabers, first seen in *Star Wars Rebels*, symbolize her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.
</Note>
