## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe. Standing at only 66 centimeters tall, this small green being lived for over 900 years and trained Jedi for over 800 of those years.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm
- **Lifespan**: 900+ years
- **Role**: Grand Master of the Jedi Order

### Notable quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Size matters not. Judge me by my size, do you?"

### Legacy

Yoda trained countless Jedi including Luke Skywalker and Count Dooku. He served on the Jedi Council during the final years of the Galactic Republic and was one of the few Jedi to survive Order 66. After the fall of the Republic, he went into exile on Dagobah where he eventually trained Luke Skywalker before becoming one with the Force.
