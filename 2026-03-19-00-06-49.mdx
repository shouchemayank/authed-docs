## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a bombing at the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order and forged her own path through the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Weapon:** Dual white lightsabers
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Yoda during the Battle of Christophsis. Initially headstrong and eager to prove herself, she matured into a skilled and compassionate leader throughout the conflict. She commanded the 501st Legion alongside Captain Rex in numerous campaigns across the galaxy.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Jedi Order, Ahsoka adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and became an intelligence operative for the growing Rebel Alliance. She played a pivotal role in connecting rebel cells and providing critical intelligence during the early days of the rebellion against the Galactic Empire.

<Note>
Ahsoka is one of the few characters to appear across multiple Star Wars eras, from the prequel-era Clone Wars through the original trilogy era and into the post-Empire period.
</Note>
