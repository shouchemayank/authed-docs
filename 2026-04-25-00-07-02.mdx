## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female from the planet Shili, best known as the former Jedi Padawan of Anakin Skywalker. Introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she quickly became a fan favorite for her sharp wit, fierce loyalty, and evolving moral compass.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsabers:** Dual white-bladed lightsabers
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, Fulcrum network

### Notable moments

- Served as a commander during the Clone Wars alongside the 501st Legion.
- Left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple.
- Survived Order 66 with the help of clone trooper Rex.
- Went on to play a pivotal role in the early Rebellion under the codename "Fulcrum."
- Confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, on Malachor.

Ahsoka's journey from apprentice to independent warrior represents one of the most compelling character arcs in the Star Wars saga.
