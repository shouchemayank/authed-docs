## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. She was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she quickly proved herself as a capable and brave warrior.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was born on the planet Shili and was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age. After being brought to the Jedi Order, she trained at the Jedi Temple before being assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his apprentice — a pairing that surprised many, given Skywalker's own unconventional approach to the Jedi Code.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic. She fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, including engagements on Christophsis, Teth, Ryloth, and Mandalore. Her experiences during the war shaped her understanding of the galaxy and the flaws within the Jedi Order.

Key accomplishments during the Clone Wars include:

- Leading the defense of Ryloth alongside Master Skywalker
- Surviving capture by Trandoshan hunters on Wasskah
- Uncovering the truth behind the Jedi Temple bombing, despite being wrongfully accused
- Commanding Republic forces during the Siege of Mandalore

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was ultimately exonerated when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was revealed, the experience left Ahsoka disillusioned. She chose to leave the Jedi Order, walking away from the only life she had known.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance, operating under the codename "Fulcrum." She continued to fight against tyranny and injustice long after the fall of the Republic, becoming one of the most respected figures in the galaxy's fight for freedom.
