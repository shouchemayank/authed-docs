## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the infamous Jango Fett, raised as his son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Origins

Boba was created on Kamino as part of an arrangement between Jango Fett and the Sith Lord Count Dooku. Unlike the millions of clone troopers produced for the Grand Army of the Republic, Boba was unmodified — he aged naturally and was raised by Jango as his own child. After Jango's death at the Battle of Geonosis, the young Boba was left to forge his own path in the galaxy.

### Career as a bounty hunter

Boba Fett quickly rose to become one of the most feared bounty hunters in the galaxy. His reputation earned him regular contracts from the Galactic Empire, most notably from Darth Vader himself. During the events of *The Empire Strikes Back*, Boba successfully tracked Han Solo to Cloud City and delivered him, frozen in carbonite, to the crime lord Jabba the Hutt.

<Note>
  Boba Fett's iconic Mandalorian armor was originally worn by his father, Jango Fett. The distinctive dents and weathering on the armor tell a story of decades of combat.
</Note>

### Legacy

Despite his apparent demise in the Sarlacc pit on Tatooine, Boba Fett survived and eventually claimed the throne of the late Jabba the Hutt. As the new Daimyo of Mos Espa, he sought to rule through respect rather than fear — a stark departure from Jabba's reign of terror. His story continues to be one of resilience and reinvention.
