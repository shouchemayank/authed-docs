## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* animated series, she has since become one of the most iconic characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at age three and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to begin her training. At fourteen, she was assigned as Padawan to Anakin Skywalker — a decision made by Yoda to help Anakin learn to let go of attachments.

### The Clone Wars

During the galactic conflict, Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and the 501st Legion across countless battlefields. She earned the nickname **"Snips"** from Anakin, while she called him **"Skyguy."** Over time, she grew from an eager, impulsive student into a capable and principled leader.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, Ahsoka chose not to return. She walked away from the only life she had known, disillusioned by the Council's failure to trust her.

### Key traits

- **Dual-wielding lightsabers** — Ahsoka is known for fighting with two white lightsabers after purifying kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor.
- **Independent thinker** — She consistently questions authority and follows her own moral compass.
- **Fulcrum** — After leaving the Order, she operated under the codename "Fulcrum" as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance.

### Legacy

Ahsoka's journey spans animation, novels, and live-action. She appeared in *Star Wars Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own Disney+ series *Ahsoka*. Voiced by Ashley Eckstein in animation and portrayed by Rosario Dawson in live action, she remains a fan favorite and a symbol of resilience in the Star Wars galaxy.
