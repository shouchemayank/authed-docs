## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who was trained by Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a crime and later acquitted, she chose to leave the Jedi Order, forging her own path in the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Weapon:** Dual white lightsabers

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic alongside her master Anakin Skywalker and Clone Captain Rex. She participated in numerous pivotal battles including the Battle of Christophsis, the Second Battle of Geonosis, and the Siege of Mandalore.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Order, Ahsoka adopted the codename "Fulcrum" and became a key intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance. She played a critical role in connecting various rebel cells across the galaxy, working closely with Senator Bail Organa.

### Confrontation with Darth Vader

One of the most defining moments in Ahsoka's story is her confrontation with her former master, now Darth Vader, on Malachor. This encounter tested her resolve and ultimately shaped her journey moving forward as she survived and continued to serve the light side of the Force.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano has become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. First introduced in the animated film *The Clone Wars* (2008), she has appeared across multiple series including *Star Wars Rebels* and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*, portrayed by Rosario Dawson.
