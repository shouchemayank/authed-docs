## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe. Originally introduced in the animated series "Star Wars: The Clone Wars", she has become a central figure across multiple Star Wars media.

### Background

Ahsoka was discovered on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, where she proved herself as a capable warrior and leader.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Lightsaber style**: Dual-wielding, with a primary blade and a shorter shoto
- **Signature colors**: White lightsabers (after leaving the Jedi Order)
- **Notable traits**: Fierce independence, strong moral compass, and unwavering loyalty to those she cares about

### Journey

Ahsoka's journey is marked by significant growth:

1. **Padawan years** - Trained under Anakin Skywalker, participating in numerous Clone Wars battles
2. **Leaving the Jedi Order** - After being falsely accused of terrorism, she left the Order even after being cleared
3. **Fulcrum** - Became a key intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance
4. **The search for Ezra** - Embarked on a quest to find the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents the best of what a Jedi can be without being bound by the rigid traditions of the Order. Her story explores themes of identity, loyalty, and finding one's own path in the Force.

> "I am no Jedi." — Ahsoka Tano
