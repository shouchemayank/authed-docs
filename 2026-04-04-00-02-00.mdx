## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She first appeared in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters across the entire franchise.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at a young age. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she trained as a youngling before being assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Jedi Grand Master Yoda.

### Role in the Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and the 501st Legion across numerous battlefields. She grew from an eager, sometimes reckless student into a skilled and resourceful leader. Key moments from this era include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement alongside Anakin
- **Siege of Mandalore** — one of the final battles of the Clone Wars, where she faced Darth Maul
- **Departure from the Jedi Order** — after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka left the Order, disillusioned with its leadership

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually joined the early Rebel Alliance under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to various rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Legacy

Ahsoka's story spans decades of the Star Wars timeline. She has appeared in *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*. Her white lightsabers and independent spirit have made her a symbol of resilience and moral clarity in the Star Wars galaxy.
