## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After leaving the Jedi Order, she became a key figure in the early rebellion against the Galactic Empire.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child on her homeworld of Shili. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she trained until being assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan at the age of fourteen during the Battle of Christophsis.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka proved herself as a capable leader and warrior. She commanded clone troopers in numerous battles, including engagements on Ryloth, Geonosis, and Mandalore. Her experiences during the war shaped her views on the Jedi Order and the Republic.

<Note>
  Ahsoka's dual white lightsabers became her signature weapons after she left the Jedi Order.
</Note>

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was ultimately proven innocent, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She chose to walk away from the Order — a decision that ultimately saved her life when Order 66 was executed.

### Key traits

- **Resourceful** — survived alone in the galaxy after leaving the Jedi
- **Compassionate** — consistently put others before herself
- **Independent** — forged her own path outside the Jedi and Sith dichotomy
- **Skilled duelist** — mastered the reverse-grip lightsaber technique

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become the operative known as "Fulcrum," helping organize rebel cells across the galaxy. She played a crucial role in the formation of the Rebel Alliance and continued to fight for justice long after the fall of the Empire.
