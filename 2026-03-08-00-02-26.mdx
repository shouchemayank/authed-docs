## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at the age of three and brought to the Jedi Temple for training. She became Anakin Skywalker's Padawan learner during the Clone Wars and proved herself to be a skilled fighter and compassionate leader.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Masters**: Anakin Skywalker, Plo Koon (discovered by)
- **Lightsaber color**: Green and yellow-green (as Padawan), white (after leaving the Order)

### Notable achievements

1. Commanded the 501st Legion alongside Anakin Skywalker
2. Left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple
3. Became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum"
4. Survived Order 66 and continued fighting against the Empire
5. Trained Sabine Wren in lightsaber combat

### Famous quote

> "I am no Jedi." — Ahsoka Tano to Darth Vader

Ahsoka's journey from Padawan to independent Force user represents one of the most compelling character arcs in Star Wars, showing that one can follow the light side of the Force without being bound to the Jedi Order.
