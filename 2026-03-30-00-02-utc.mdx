## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a bombing at the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order and forged her own path through the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** White (after purifying Inquisitor kyber crystals)

### Role in the Clone Wars

Ahsoka was assigned as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan by Jedi Master Yoda, partly to teach Anakin responsibility. She proved herself as a capable leader and warrior across many campaigns, earning the respect of clone troopers under her command, particularly Captain Rex of the 501st Legion.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Order, Ahsoka adopted the alias "Ashla" and lived in hiding during the rise of the Empire. She eventually joined the growing rebellion and operated under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Legacy

Ahsoka stands out as one of the most compelling characters in Star Wars lore. Her journey from a young, eager Padawan to an independent Force wielder who walks her own path reflects themes of growth, disillusionment, and resilience that resonate across the franchise.
