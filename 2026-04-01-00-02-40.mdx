## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is one of the most powerful Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe, known for his exceptional combat skills and unwavering dedication to the Jedi Order. He served as a senior member of the Jedi High Council during the final years of the Galactic Republic.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Human
- **Homeworld:** Haruun Kal
- **Lightsaber color:** Purple — the only Jedi in the prequel trilogy to wield one
- **Fighting style:** Vaapad, a variant of Form VII that channels the user's inner darkness into a weapon of light
- **Portrayed by:** Samuel L. Jackson

### Role in the Star Wars saga

Mace Windu played a pivotal role during the Clone Wars, leading Republic forces in several key battles. He was among the first Jedi to confront Chancellor Palpatine after discovering his true identity as Darth Sidious. During that confrontation, Windu managed to overpower the Sith Lord in lightsaber combat — a testament to his extraordinary skill — but was ultimately betrayed by Anakin Skywalker, leading to his fall from the window of the Chancellor's office on Coruscant.

### Legacy

Windu's creation of the Vaapad fighting style remains one of the most notable contributions to Jedi combat philosophy. His willingness to walk the line between light and dark — using aggressive emotions without succumbing to them — made him a unique figure among the Jedi. His defeat at the hands of Palpatine and Anakin marked a turning point that triggered Order 66 and the fall of the Jedi Order.
