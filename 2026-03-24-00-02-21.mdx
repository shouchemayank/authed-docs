## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the legendary bounty hunter Jango Fett, raised as his son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Background

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back*, where Darth Vader hired him alongside other bounty hunters to track down Han Solo and the Millennium Falcon. He successfully delivered Solo, frozen in carbonite, to the crime lord Jabba the Hutt.

### Key traits

- **Armor:** Boba wears customized Mandalorian armor inherited from his father, equipped with a jetpack, wrist-mounted weaponry, and a distinctive T-shaped visor.
- **Ship:** His signature starship, *Slave I* (later known as *Firespray*), is one of the most recognizable vessels in the galaxy.
- **Reputation:** Known across the galaxy as one of the most feared and effective bounty hunters, Boba commands respect from both the criminal underworld and the Empire.

### Survival and legacy

Though presumed dead after falling into the Sarlacc pit on Tatooine during the events of *Return of the Jedi*, Boba Fett survived. He later reclaimed his armor and took over Jabba the Hutt's criminal empire, ruling as the Daimyo of Mos Espa alongside the assassin Fennec Shand.

<Note>
Boba Fett's story spans across multiple Star Wars films and series, including *The Mandalorian* and *The Book of Boba Fett*.
</Note>
