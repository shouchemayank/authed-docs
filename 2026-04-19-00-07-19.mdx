## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*, she grew from a headstrong young apprentice into one of the most respected figures in the galaxy.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. At fourteen, she was assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his Padawan — a pairing that surprised both of them but ultimately shaped their destinies.

### The Clone Wars

During the conflict, Ahsoka fought in numerous battles across the galaxy, earning the nickname **Snips** from her master. She commanded clone troopers, developed her dual-wielding lightsaber technique, and matured into a capable leader. Her experiences during the war gave her a perspective on the Jedi Order that few others shared.

### Departure from the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though eventually exonerated when the true culprit was revealed, the ordeal shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the only life she had known.

<Note>
Ahsoka's departure from the Jedi Order is considered one of the most pivotal moments in Clone Wars storytelling, fundamentally changing both her character arc and Anakin Skywalker's path toward the dark side.
</Note>

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename **Fulcrum**, providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy. She later confronted her former master — now Darth Vader — in a dramatic duel on Malachor. Her story continued through the era of the New Republic, where she searched for the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger.

### Notable traits

- **Lightsaber style:** Dual-wielding with a standard lightsaber and a shorter shoto blade, later carrying white lightsabers symbolizing her independence from both Jedi and Sith
- **Species:** Togruta, recognizable by her montrals and head-tails (lekku)
- **Key relationships:** Anakin Skywalker (master), Captain Rex (close ally), Sabine Wren (apprentice)
- **Signature phrase:** "I am no Jedi."
