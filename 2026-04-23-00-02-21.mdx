## Grogu

Grogu, often referred to as "The Child" or "Baby Yoda" by fans, is a Force-sensitive foundling from the same mysterious species as Jedi Master Yoda. He first appeared in the Disney+ series "The Mandalorian" and quickly became a cultural phenomenon.

### Background

Grogu was born around 41 BBY and was being trained at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant during the final days of the Clone Wars. He survived Order 66 when an unknown ally smuggled him out of the temple, and his memories of the events that followed were largely suppressed.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Unknown (same as Yoda and Yaddle)
- **Age**: Approximately 50 years old during the events of "The Mandalorian"
- **Force abilities**: Telekinesis, Force healing, and mind influence
- **Notable traits**: Curious, mischievous, and deeply attached to his adoptive father Din Djarin

### Journey

Grogu's journey is one of rediscovery and connection:

1. **Jedi youngling** - Trained briefly at the Jedi Temple before Order 66
2. **Bounty target** - Hunted by Imperial remnants seeking his Force abilities
3. **Rescue by the Mandalorian** - Found and protected by Din Djarin
4. **Training with Luke Skywalker** - Briefly studied at Luke's new Jedi academy
5. **Return to Din Djarin** - Chose the path of the Mandalorian over the Jedi

### Legacy

Grogu represents a new generation of Force users who blend traditions rather than adhering to a single path. His bond with Din Djarin showcases that family is forged through choice and devotion, not just blood or creed.

> "This is the Way." — The Mandalorian creed, now shared with Grogu
