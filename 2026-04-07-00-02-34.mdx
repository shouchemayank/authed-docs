## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a crime and later acquitted, she chose to leave the Jedi Order — a decision that profoundly shaped her path and identity.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliations:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** White (post-Jedi Order)

### Role in the Clone Wars

As a young Padawan, Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Yoda. She quickly proved herself as a capable and brave warrior, earning the nickname "Snips" from her master. Throughout the conflict, she led troops in major battles on Christophsis, Ryloth, and Mandalore.

### Life after the Jedi Order

After leaving the Order, Ahsoka went into hiding during the rise of the Empire. She eventually resurfaced as the covert operative "Fulcrum," providing intelligence to various rebel cells. Her efforts were instrumental in the formation of the early Rebel Alliance.

### Legacy

Ahsoka stands as one of the most significant characters in the Star Wars universe. She represents the idea that you can walk away from an institution and still fight for what is right. Her journey from Padawan to independent Force-wielder resonates with fans as a story of growth, resilience, and moral clarity.
