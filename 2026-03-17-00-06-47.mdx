## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon as a young child and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant to be trained. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, serving as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Weapon:** Dual lightsabers (white blades)

### Early life

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, a pairing that surprised both of them. Despite a rocky start, the two developed a strong bond throughout the Clone Wars, with Ahsoka growing from an impulsive youngling into a capable and wise leader.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was ultimately proven innocent when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true culprit, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order — a decision that deeply affected both her and Anakin.

### Legacy

Ahsoka went on to become a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance, operating under the codename **Fulcrum**. She provided intelligence to various rebel cells and eventually confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, in a dramatic duel on Malachor. Her story spans across *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own series *Ahsoka*.
