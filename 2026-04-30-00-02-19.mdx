## Featured character: Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili, best known as the former Padawan of Anakin Skywalker. She first appeared in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the galaxy far, far away.

### Quick facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (formerly), Rebel Alliance, Fulcrum network
- **Lightsabers:** Two white-bladed lightsabers
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker

### Notable moments

- Walked away from the Jedi Order after being wrongly accused of a bombing.
- Survived Order 66 alongside the clone trooper Captain Rex.
- Took on the codename **Fulcrum** while supporting the early rebellion.
- Confronted Darth Vader on Malachor and later helped Grogu reconnect with his past.

Ahsoka's journey embodies resilience, independence, and the idea that the path of the light side is not always defined by a single institution.
