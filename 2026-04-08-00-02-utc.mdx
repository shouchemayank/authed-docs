## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi Padawan who served under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. After being falsely accused of a crime and later exonerated, she chose to leave the Jedi Order — a decision that shaped the rest of her life.

| Detail       | Info                          |
| ------------ | ----------------------------- |
| Species      | Togruta                       |

| Affiliation  | Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance |
| Master       | Anakin Skywalker              |
| First appearance | *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008) |

### Why she matters

- **Bridging eras** — Ahsoka connects the prequel trilogy, the original trilogy, and the sequel-era timeline through appearances in *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own series *Ahsoka*.
- **Independence from the Jedi** — She is one of the few Force-sensitive characters to walk away from the Jedi Order on her own terms, offering a unique perspective on the Force.
- **Fan favorite** — Originally met with skepticism when introduced as Anakin's apprentice, Ahsoka grew into one of the most popular characters in all of Star Wars thanks to her resilience, wit, and moral compass.
