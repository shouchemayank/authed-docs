## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the Star Wars universe. Standing at only 66 centimeters tall, this ancient being served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order for centuries before the fall of the Republic.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown (officially listed as "Yoda's species")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm
- **Lifespan**: Approximately 900 years
- **Lightsaber color**: Green

### Notable quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Luminous beings are we, not this crude matter."

### Abilities

Yoda was renowned for his mastery of the Force, including:

- Telekinesis - able to lift objects many times his own size
- Force visions - could glimpse possible futures
- Lightsaber combat - despite his size, one of the greatest duelists
- Force healing - could heal injuries through the Force
- Connection with the cosmic Force - achieved immortality as a Force ghost

### Legacy

After surviving Order 66, Yoda exiled himself to Dagobah where he trained Luke Skywalker before becoming one with the Force. His teachings continued to guide Jedi long after his death.
