## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is a senior member of the Jedi High Council and one of the most powerful Jedi in the history of the Galactic Republic. Known for his stoic demeanor and unwavering commitment to the Jedi Order, he serves as a key figure during the final years of the Republic.

### Notable traits

- **Lightsaber color:** Purple — Windu is the only known Jedi to wield an amethyst-bladed lightsaber, reflecting his unique connection to the Force.
- **Fighting style:** He is the sole master of Vaapad, the seventh and most aggressive form of lightsaber combat. Vaapad channels a fighter's inner darkness into a weapon of light, making it both powerful and dangerous.
- **Force abilities:** Windu possesses an extraordinary ability called "shatterpoint," which allows him to perceive fault lines in people, situations, and objects — and exploit them.

### Key moments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery of Anakin Skywalker">
    Windu is among the first to express skepticism about training young Anakin, sensing the boy's deep-seated fear and attachment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Battle of Geonosis">
    He leads a strike team of over 200 Jedi into the Geonosian arena, igniting the first battle of the Clone Wars.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confrontation with Palpatine">
    Windu confronts Chancellor Palpatine after learning his true identity as Darth Sidious. He defeats Sidious in combat but is ultimately betrayed by Anakin Skywalker and thrown from the window of the Chancellor's office.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Legacy

Mace Windu represents the ideals of the Jedi Order at their peak — discipline, power, and an uncompromising sense of justice. His fall marks the beginning of the end for the Jedi and the rise of the Galactic Empire.

<Note>
  Mace Windu is portrayed by Samuel L. Jackson across the Star Wars prequel trilogy: *The Phantom Menace* (1999), *Attack of the Clones* (2002), and *Revenge of the Sith* (2005).
</Note>
