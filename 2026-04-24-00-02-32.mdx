## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta former Jedi introduced in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* as the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker. Known for her dual lightsabers and reverse-grip combat style, she quickly became a fan favorite for her wit, independence, and moral clarity.

### Key details

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Signature weapons:** Dual white lightsabers

### Story highlights

- Left the Jedi Order after being wrongfully accused of a bombing, a pivotal moment that foreshadowed the Order's decline.
- Survived Order 66 with the help of former clone trooper Captain Rex.
- Reappeared across *Star Wars Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, *The Book of Boba Fett*, and her own series *Ahsoka*.
- Adopted the title "Ahsoka the White" after her confrontation with Darth Vader on Malachor.

Ahsoka's journey from eager apprentice to wise, self-reliant warrior represents one of the most complete character arcs in all of *Star Wars*.
