## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in the animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008), she has since become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at a young age. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, where she trained as a youngling before being assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his Padawan by Grandmaster Yoda.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and Captain Rex across numerous battlefields. She proved herself as a capable and resourceful warrior, earning the nickname **Snips** from her master — a reference to her snippy attitude. Key moments during this period include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement as a Padawan
- **Siege of Mandalore** — one of the final battles of the Clone Wars, where she faced Darth Maul
- **Departure from the Jedi Order** — after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka chose to leave the Order even after being exonerated

### After the fall of the Republic

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding. She eventually resurfaced as the Rebel intelligence operative known as **Fulcrum**, providing critical support to early rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Lightsaber style

Ahsoka is known for wielding two lightsabers — a standard-length saber and a shorter shoto blade. During her time as a Jedi, her blades were green and yellow-green. After leaving the Order and purifying kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor, she carried distinctive **white lightsabers**, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano's story spans animation, novels, and live-action television. Portrayed by Rosario Dawson in *The Mandalorian* and the *Ahsoka* series, she continues to play a pivotal role in the broader Star Wars narrative — searching for the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger and confronting threats from beyond the known galaxy.
