## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple to be trained. She became the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliations**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Weapon**: Dual lightsabers (white blades)

### Story highlights

Ahsoka left the Jedi Order after being wrongfully accused of a bombing at the Jedi Temple. Though eventually cleared, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She later became a key figure in the Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum," providing intelligence to rebel cells across the galaxy.

After the fall of the Empire, Ahsoka continued her journey, searching for Grand Admiral Thrawn and her friend Ezra Bridger in the unknown regions of the galaxy.

### Why Ahsoka matters

Ahsoka represents growth and independence. She shows that one can walk away from rigid institutions while still holding onto core values. Her journey from eager Padawan to wise warrior resonates with fans who appreciate character development across decades of storytelling.
