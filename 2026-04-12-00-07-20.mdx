## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter and clone of the legendary Jango Fett, he carved out a fearsome reputation across the galaxy.

### Origins

Boba Fett was created as an unaltered clone of the bounty hunter Jango Fett on the ocean planet Kamino. Unlike the millions of clone troopers produced for the Republic, Boba was raised as Jango's son. After Jango's death at the hands of Jedi Master Mace Windu during the Battle of Geonosis, the young Boba was left to fend for himself, eventually following in his father's footsteps as a bounty hunter.

### Career highlights

- **Hired by Darth Vader** to track down the Millennium Falcon and its crew after the Battle of Hoth
- **Captured Han Solo** and delivered him, frozen in carbonite, to Jabba the Hutt
- **Survived the Sarlacc Pit** on Tatooine, despite being knocked into it during the rescue of Han Solo at the Great Pit of Carkoon
- **Claimed the throne of Jabba's Palace**, ruling Tatooine's criminal underworld as Daimyo alongside Fennec Shand

### Equipment

Boba Fett's distinctive Mandalorian armor included a range of built-in weaponry:

- Wrist-mounted flamethrower and rocket launcher
- Jetpack with integrated missile
- EE-3 carbine blaster rifle
- Knee-pad rocket dart launchers
- Fibercord whip

### Legacy

Boba Fett's influence extends far beyond the original trilogy. His character helped inspire the entire Mandalorian culture that has become central to Star Wars storytelling, from the animated series *The Clone Wars* to the live-action series *The Mandalorian* and *The Book of Boba Fett*.
