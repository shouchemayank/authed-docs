## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a former Jedi Padawan who was trained by Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, a decision that shaped her path as a force-wielder outside traditional Jedi constraints.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliations**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance, New Republic
- **Lightsaber color**: Originally green, later white (purified kyber crystals)

### Notable appearances

Ahsoka appears across multiple Star Wars media:

1. **The Clone Wars** - As Anakin's Padawan
2. **Rebels** - As a key figure in the early Rebellion
3. **The Mandalorian** - Searching for Grand Admiral Thrawn
4. **Ahsoka** - Her own live-action series

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents a unique perspective in the Star Wars universe—someone trained in the Jedi ways who chose to walk her own path. Her story explores themes of identity, loyalty, and redemption outside the traditional light-versus-dark narrative.
