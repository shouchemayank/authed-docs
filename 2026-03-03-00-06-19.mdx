## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who began her journey as a Jedi Padawan under Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Known for her distinctive montrals and lekku, white facial markings, and dual white lightsabers, she became one of the most skilled warriors in the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master**: Anakin Skywalker
- **Nickname**: Snips (given by Anakin)

### Journey

Ahsoka left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple. This pivotal moment shaped her path as she continued to fight for justice outside the traditional Jedi structure. During the era of the Galactic Empire, she became a key figure in the early Rebellion under the codename "Fulcrum."

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents growth, resilience, and the ability to forge your own path. Her character arc spans from an eager young Padawan to a wise and powerful Force user who operates outside traditional boundaries while staying true to her principles.
