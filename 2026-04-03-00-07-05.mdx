## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, earning the nickname "Snips" for her snippy attitude.

### Early years

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, much to Anakin's initial reluctance. Over time, the two formed a strong bond as master and apprentice, fighting side by side through some of the most pivotal battles of the Clone Wars.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was eventually exonerated when Barriss Offee was revealed as the true culprit, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Order, leaving Anakin behind.

### The Rebellion era

Ahsoka resurfaced years later under the codename "Fulcrum," working as an intelligence operative for the growing rebel movement. She played a key role in organizing rebel cells across the galaxy and eventually confronted her former master — now Darth Vader — in a dramatic lightsaber duel on the Sith temple on Malachor.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano represents growth, resilience, and moral clarity in the Star Wars saga. She is one of the few characters whose journey spans the prequel era, the original trilogy timeline, and beyond into the New Republic period. Whether wielding her signature white lightsabers or guiding the next generation of heroes, Ahsoka remains a fan favorite across all Star Wars media.
