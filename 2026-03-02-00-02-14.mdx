## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner to Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. Originally introduced in the animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008), she has since become one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars universe.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** Originally green and yellow-green, later white

### Character journey

Ahsoka began as a young, eager Padawan assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Master Yoda. Throughout the Clone Wars, she grew from an impulsive learner into a skilled warrior and leader. After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she left the Jedi Order—a decision that would ultimately save her from Order 66.

Following the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka operated under the codename "Fulcrum" as an intelligence operative for the growing rebellion against the Empire. She later confronted her former master, now Darth Vader, in a dramatic duel on Malachor.
