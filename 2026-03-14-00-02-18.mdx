## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A legendary Jedi Master, he trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a pivotal role in the fate of the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

### Legacy

Yoda served as the Grand Master of the Jedi Order during the final years of the Republic. Known for his wisdom, combat prowess, and distinctive speech pattern, he trained generations of Jedi including Count Dooku and Luke Skywalker.

After surviving Order 66, Yoda went into exile on Dagobah, where he spent his final years before training Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Force.

<Note>
"Do or do not. There is no try." — Yoda
</Note>

### Abilities

Yoda was considered one of the most powerful Force users in galactic history:

- Master of all lightsaber combat forms
- Exceptionally strong connection to the Force
- Ability to absorb and redirect Force lightning
- Precognitive visions through the Force
