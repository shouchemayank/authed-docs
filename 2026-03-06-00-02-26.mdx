## Yoda

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. As a legendary Jedi Master, he trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a crucial role in the fate of the galaxy.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Unknown
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic
- **Lightsaber color**: Green

### Notable traits

Yoda is known for his distinctive speech pattern, inverting typical sentence structure. His wisdom and mastery of the Force made him one of the most powerful Jedi to ever live.

> "Do or do not. There is no try." — Yoda

### Role in Star Wars

Yoda served on the Jedi Council and was instrumental in training Luke Skywalker on Dagobah. He survived the fall of the Jedi Order during Order 66 and lived in exile until his death, becoming one with the Force.
