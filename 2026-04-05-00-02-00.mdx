## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who first appeared in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker. Over time, she became one of the most iconic characters in the entire Star Wars franchise.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. During the Clone Wars, Jedi Grand Master Yoda assigned her as Anakin Skywalker's apprentice — a pairing that would shape both of their destinies.

### Key moments

- **The Clone Wars** — Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and the 501st Legion across countless battlefields, earning the nickname "Snips" from her master.
- **Leaving the Jedi Order** — After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka chose to leave the Order even after her name was cleared, disillusioned with the Council's lack of trust.
- **The Siege of Mandalore** — Ahsoka led Republic forces alongside Bo-Katan Kryze to capture the renegade Sith Lord Maul on Mandalore, just as Order 66 was executed.
- **Rebellion era** — Operating under the codename "Fulcrum," Ahsoka served as an intelligence operative for the early Rebel Alliance, providing critical support to groups like the Ghost crew.
- **Confrontation with Darth Vader** — In one of the most emotionally charged moments in Star Wars, Ahsoka dueled her former master in the Sith Temple on Malachor.

### Legacy

Ahsoka Tano represents growth, resilience, and moral conviction. She walks a unique path — no longer a Jedi, yet firmly committed to the light side of the Force. Her story spans animation, novels, and live-action, with Rosario Dawson portraying her in *The Mandalorian* and the *Ahsoka* series on Disney+.

<Note>
  Ahsoka's white lightsabers are purified kyber crystals taken from an Inquisitor's weapon, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.
</Note>
