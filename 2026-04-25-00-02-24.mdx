## Grogu

Grogu, affectionately known as "The Child" or "Baby Yoda," is a Force-sensitive foundling of the same species as Jedi Master Yoda. First introduced in "The Mandalorian," Grogu quickly became a cultural phenomenon and a beloved character in the Star Wars galaxy.

### Background

Grogu was born around 41 BBY and was being raised at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant during the final days of the Galactic Republic. He survived Order 66 when someone rescued him from the Temple and hid him away, protecting him from the Empire's purge of Force-sensitive individuals.

### Key characteristics

- **Species**: Unknown (same as Yoda and Yaddle)
- **Age**: Approximately 50 years old during "The Mandalorian"
- **Notable traits**: Large ears, big black eyes, and a gentle demeanor
- **Force abilities**: Telekinesis, Force healing, and mind influence

### Journey

Grogu's story has unfolded across multiple chapters:

1. **Rescue from the Empire** - Found by the Mandalorian bounty hunter Din Djarin, who refused to hand him over to Imperial remnants
2. **Bond with Din Djarin** - Formed a powerful father-son relationship with his Mandalorian protector
3. **Training with Luke Skywalker** - Briefly trained at Luke's new Jedi academy
4. **Choosing his path** - Chose the Mandalorian way over the Jedi path, becoming a Mandalorian apprentice

### Legacy

Grogu represents hope, innocence, and the bonds that form in the most unlikely places. His story bridges the gap between the fall of the Jedi Order and the rise of new traditions, showing that the Force finds its way forward through unexpected connections.

> "This is the way." — The Mandalorian creed that now guides Grogu's journey
