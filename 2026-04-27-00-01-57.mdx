## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta Force-sensitive who first appeared in *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* as the Padawan apprentice to Anakin Skywalker. Known for her dual lightsabers and unwavering moral compass, she becomes one of the most beloved characters in the saga.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Lightsabers:** Wields two white-bladed lightsabers after leaving the Jedi Order
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker

### Notable arcs

After being wrongfully accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, Ahsoka leaves the Jedi Order. She later resurfaces as the Rebel agent codenamed "Fulcrum" and plays a pivotal role in the events of *Star Wars Rebels* and *The Mandalorian*.
