## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is the unaltered clone of the infamous bounty hunter Jango Fett, raised as his son.

### Key facts

- **Species**: Human (clone)
- **Homeworld**: Kamino
- **Affiliation**: Bounty Hunters' Guild, Hutt Clan
- **Armor**: Customized Mandalorian armor inherited from his father

### Notable appearances

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back* (1980), where he was hired by Darth Vader to track down Han Solo. His distinctive green and red Mandalorian armor, jetpack, and silent demeanor made him an instant fan favorite.

After seemingly meeting his end in the Sarlacc pit in *Return of the Jedi*, Boba Fett returned in *The Mandalorian* series and later starred in his own show, *The Book of Boba Fett*, where he takes over Jabba the Hutt's criminal empire on Tatooine.

### Famous quote

> "He's no good to me dead." — Boba Fett, referring to Han Solo
