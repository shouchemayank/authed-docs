## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a fan-favorite character in the Star Wars universe, first introduced in the animated series *The Clone Wars* as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan learner.

### Background

- **Species**: Togruta
- **Homeworld**: Shili
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Notable traits**: White lightsabers, distinctive head-tails (lekku), facial markings

### Why fans love her

Ahsoka represents growth and resilience. She left the Jedi Order after being falsely accused, yet continued to fight for justice. Her character arc demonstrates that being a hero isn't about titles—it's about choices.

<Note>
  Ahsoka's white lightsabers symbolize her independence from both the Jedi and Sith, walking her own path in the Force.
</Note>
