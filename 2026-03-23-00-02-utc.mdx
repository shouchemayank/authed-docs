## Mace Windu

Mace Windu is a senior member of the Jedi Council and one of the most skilled lightsaber duelists in the Star Wars galaxy. Known for his distinctive purple lightsaber, he is the only Jedi in the prequel trilogy to wield this unique blade color.

### Background

Windu was born on the planet Haruun Kal and was identified as Force-sensitive at a young age. He rose through the ranks of the Jedi Order to become one of its most respected leaders, serving alongside Yoda on the Jedi High Council during the final decades of the Galactic Republic.

### Combat style

Windu is the creator and sole master of Vaapad, the most aggressive form of lightsaber combat (Form VII). This fighting style channels the user's inner darkness into a weapon of light, making it extremely dangerous for anyone who lacks discipline. His mastery of Vaapad allowed him to defeat Darth Sidious in direct combat — one of the few Jedi ever to accomplish this.

### Legacy

Despite his fall, Mace Windu remains one of the most powerful Jedi to have ever lived. His wisdom, combat prowess, and unwavering commitment to the Jedi Code made him a pillar of the Order during its final years.
