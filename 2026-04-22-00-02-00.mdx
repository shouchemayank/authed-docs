## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, assigned to him by Jedi Grand Master Yoda in hopes that teaching responsibility would help Anakin grow.

### Early life and the Clone Wars

Ahsoka served as a commander in the Grand Army of the Republic alongside Anakin and Captain Rex. Over the course of the war, she developed from an impulsive young learner into a skilled and strategic leader. She participated in key battles across the galaxy, including the sieges of Mandalore and Christophsis.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually exonerated when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, Ahsoka chose not to return. She walked away from the only life she had known, disillusioned by the Council's failure to trust her.

### The rebellion era

Under the codename "Fulcrum," Ahsoka became an intelligence operative for the growing rebel network. She provided critical support to various rebel cells, including the crew of the starship *Ghost*. Her confrontation with Darth Vader on Malachor remains one of the most emotionally charged moments in Star Wars storytelling.

<Note>
Ahsoka is one of the few characters whose story spans the prequel era, the original trilogy era, and beyond into the New Republic period.
</Note>

### Key traits

- **Dual white lightsabers** — After leaving the Jedi Order, Ahsoka wielded a pair of white-bladed lightsabers, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.
- **Strategic mind** — Known for creative battlefield tactics that often caught enemies off guard.
- **Moral compass** — Consistently guided by a strong sense of justice, even when it put her at odds with authority.

### Legacy

Ahsoka's journey from Padawan to independent Force wielder has made her one of the most beloved characters in the Star Wars franchise. First introduced in the animated *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* film in 2008, she went on to appear in *Star Wars Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*.
