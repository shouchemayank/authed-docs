## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Affiliation:** Jedi Order (former), Rebel Alliance
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Lightsaber color:** Originally green, later white

### Notable achievements

Ahsoka played a crucial role in many pivotal moments throughout the Clone Wars. After leaving the Jedi Order due to false accusations, she eventually became a key figure in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename "Fulcrum."

Her journey represents one of the most compelling character arcs in Star Wars, showing growth from a headstrong Padawan to a wise and independent Force user who walks her own path outside the traditional Jedi and Sith dichotomy.
