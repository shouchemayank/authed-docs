## Boba Fett

Boba Fett is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A Mandalorian bounty hunter, he is a clone of the infamous Jango Fett and was raised as Jango's son on the ocean planet of Kamino.

### Background

Boba Fett first appeared in *The Empire Strikes Back*, where he was hired by Darth Vader to track down the Millennium Falcon and capture Han Solo. His distinctive Mandalorian armor, jetpack, and stoic demeanor quickly made him a fan favorite despite his limited screen time.

### Key facts

- **Species:** Human (unaltered clone)
- **Homeworld:** Kamino
- **Affiliation:** Bounty Hunters' Guild
- **Armor:** Customized Mandalorian beskar armor, inherited from Jango Fett
- **Ship:** *Slave I* (later known as *Firespray*)
- **Notable skills:** Expert marksman, hand-to-hand combat, jetpack warfare, tracking

### Legacy

After surviving the Sarlacc pit on Tatooine, Boba Fett went on to claim the throne of the late Jabba the Hutt, becoming the Daimyo of Mos Espa. His story is explored in *The Book of Boba Fett*, where he transitions from a feared bounty hunter to a leader trying to rule with respect rather than fear.

Boba Fett's journey from a silent, mysterious antagonist to a fully realized protagonist remains one of the most compelling character arcs in the Star Wars saga.
