## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who first appeared in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars*. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan learner, she has since grown into one of the most prominent figures in Star Wars storytelling.

### Early life and training

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. She was assigned as Padawan to Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, a pairing that surprised both of them. Over time, the two developed a strong bond built on mutual respect and shared battlefield experience.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was eventually cleared when the true culprit — fellow Padawan Barriss Offee — was exposed, Ahsoka chose to leave the Jedi Order. This decision marked a turning point in her character, setting her apart from traditional Jedi.

### Role in the Rebellion

Following Order 66 and the fall of the Republic, Ahsoka went into hiding but eventually became a key operative in the early Rebel Alliance under the codename **Fulcrum**. She provided intelligence to various rebel cells, including the crew of the *Ghost* in *Star Wars Rebels*.

### Key traits

- **Dual white lightsabers** — After leaving the Jedi Order, Ahsoka wielded a pair of white-bladed lightsabers, symbolizing her neutrality.
- **Strong moral compass** — She consistently chose to do what was right, even when it meant defying authority.
- **Skilled combatant** — Ahsoka is one of the most capable duelists in the Star Wars canon, having held her own against opponents like Darth Maul and Darth Vader.

### Live-action appearances

Ahsoka made her live-action debut in *The Mandalorian* Season 2, portrayed by Rosario Dawson. She later starred in her own series, *Ahsoka*, which continued her search for the missing Jedi Ezra Bridger.
