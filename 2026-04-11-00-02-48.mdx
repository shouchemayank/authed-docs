## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant at a young age. She became the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars, a pairing that would shape both of their destinies.

### Early life and the Clone Wars

Ahsoka was assigned to Anakin Skywalker by Jedi Grand Master Yoda, much to Anakin's initial reluctance. Over time, the two developed a strong bond as master and apprentice, fighting side by side across numerous battlefields during the Clone Wars. She earned the nickname **Snips** from Anakin due to her snippy attitude, while she affectionately called him **Skyguy**.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Jedi Order and put on trial. Though she was eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was exposed, the experience shattered her trust in the Order. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Jedi, one of the most pivotal moments in Clone Wars history.

### Life after the Jedi

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding before eventually joining the early rebellion against Imperial rule. Operating under the codename **Fulcrum**, she provided intelligence to various rebel cells, including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Key traits

- **Dual-wielding lightsabers** — Ahsoka is known for fighting with two white lightsabers, which she purified from red Inquisitor kyber crystals.
- **Independence** — Her decision to leave the Jedi Order demonstrated a willingness to follow her own moral compass.
- **Loyalty** — Despite leaving the Order, she never stopped caring about the people she fought alongside.
- **Wisdom** — By the time of the New Republic era, Ahsoka had grown into one of the most knowledgeable Force users in the galaxy.

### Appearances

Ahsoka has appeared across multiple Star Wars media, including *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, *The Mandalorian*, and her own live-action series *Ahsoka*. She is voiced by Ashley Eckstein in animation and portrayed by Rosario Dawson in live action.
