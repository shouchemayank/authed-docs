## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta from the planet Shili who became one of the most significant figures in the Star Wars galaxy. Originally introduced as Anakin Skywalker's Padawan during the Clone Wars, she grew from an eager young learner into a seasoned warrior and leader.

### Early life and the Jedi Order

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon at a young age and brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training. At the age of 14, she was assigned as a Padawan to Anakin Skywalker — a decision made by Yoda to help Anakin learn to let go of attachments. The master-apprentice pair quickly formed a strong bond as they fought side by side throughout the Clone Wars.

### Leaving the Jedi Order

After being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple hangar, Ahsoka was expelled from the Order and put on trial. Although she was eventually cleared when the true culprit, Barriss Offee, was revealed, the experience shattered her trust in the Jedi Council. She made the difficult decision to walk away from the Order, becoming one of the few characters to leave the Jedi voluntarily.

### Life after the Jedi

Following Order 66 and the rise of the Empire, Ahsoka went into hiding under the alias "Ashla." She eventually joined the early rebellion against the Empire, operating under the codename "Fulcrum" and providing intelligence to various rebel cells. During this period, she wielded a pair of white lightsabers, symbolizing her independence from both the Jedi and the Sith.

### Key characteristics

- **Species:** Togruta
- **Homeworld:** Shili
- **Lightsaber color:** White (dual-wielded)
- **Notable aliases:** Ashla, Fulcrum
- **Master:** Anakin Skywalker
- **Key trait:** A strong moral compass that operates independently of any single organization

<Note>
Ahsoka Tano first appeared in the 2008 animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise, appearing across multiple animated series, novels, and live-action shows.
