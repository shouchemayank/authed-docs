## Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano is a Togruta female who served as the Padawan learner of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone Wars. She was first introduced in the animated film *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* (2008) and has since become one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

### Early life

Ahsoka was discovered by Jedi Master Plo Koon on her homeworld of Shili at the age of three. She was brought to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant for training and was later assigned to Anakin Skywalker as his apprentice by Jedi Grand Master Yoda.

### The Clone Wars

During the Clone Wars, Ahsoka fought alongside Anakin and Captain Rex across numerous battlefields. She earned the nickname **"Snips"** from Anakin due to her snippy attitude, while she called him **"Skyguy"** in return.

Key moments during the war include:

- **Battle of Christophsis** — her first major engagement as a Padawan
- **Siege of Mandalore** — her final mission during the Clone Wars, where she faced Darth Maul
- **Departure from the Jedi Order** — after being falsely accused of bombing the Jedi Temple, she chose to leave the Order even after being acquitted

### After the fall of the Republic

Ahsoka survived Order 66 with the help of Captain Rex and went into hiding. She eventually joined the early Rebel Alliance under the codename **"Fulcrum"**, providing intelligence to various rebel cells including the crew of the *Ghost*.

### Legacy

Ahsoka represents growth beyond the traditional Jedi path. She walks the line between light and dark, serving the Force without being bound by the rigid doctrines of the Jedi Order. Her white lightsabers symbolize her independence and neutrality.
